Workshop Agenda

What we'll cover in 4 hours.

A fast-paced mini MasterClass in two parts — eight areas of Liberty that drive the most impact on your store. Real workflows, live demos, and the hot integrations rolling out in Liberty Cloud.

Part 1 · Intake

Item intake & AI Pricing

Drop-offs, the Item Entry App, the Pricing Guide, and AI Pricing — get items in the door faster and priced smarter from day one.

Part 1 · POS

Point of Sale

The new Refund Sale, Add Item, Item Lookup, and the small POS habits that keep checkout fast and consistent across the team.

Part 1 · Money

Modern payouts

ACH transfers via ResaleWallet, gift cards, and barcode-sticker payouts — pay consignors faster, with less paper and fewer errors.

Part 1 · Admin

Admin workflows

Merging accounts, consignor contracts, and the Kiosk app — clean back-office processes that prevent payout headaches later.

Part 2 · Accounts

Account management

Retail variants, contracts, letters, and pick lists — the daily account workflows that scale with you as your store grows.

Part 2 · Marketing

Marketing stack

Klaviyo, ResaleRewards, and Constant Contact — turn Liberty customer data into email, SMS, and loyalty programs that drive repeat visits.

Part 2 · Online

reCommerce

Shopify and eBay integrations — automate online listings, manage markdowns, and keep online and in-store inventory in sync.

Part 2 · Power tools

Power-user extras

Inventory Reconciliation, the built-in word processor, and the Email Manager — the underused features that quietly save hours every week.

This is a fast-paced 4-hour tour. Want to go deeper on any of these areas? Resaleworld's 3-day MasterClass takes the full life of an item end-to-end — virtual or in-person at our Orlando HQ.

Frequently asked

A few quick answers before you sign up. If you don't see your question, reach out — we're happy to help.

Is the Liberty Seminar Course really free?
Yes — there's no cost to attend the seminar itself. Travel, lodging, and any NARTS Conference passes are handled separately by you and NARTS.
Do I need to be a current Liberty customer to attend?
Not at all. The seminar is open to current Liberty users and to anyone evaluating Liberty for their store.
Where is the seminar held?
Each year we host the seminar inside the NARTS conference hotel or at a nearby venue within walking distance. The exact location, room, and start time are shared with registered attendees by email once NARTS publishes the official conference details.
What topics will be covered?
It's a 4-hour mini MasterClass in two parts, covering eight areas of Liberty: item intake (drop-offs, the Item Entry App, Pricing Guide, AI Pricing), point of sale, modern payouts (ACH, gift cards, barcode stickers), admin workflows (account merges, consignor contracts, Kiosk app), account management (retail variants, contracts, letters, pick lists), the marketing stack (Klaviyo, ResaleRewards, Constant Contact), reCommerce (Shopify, eBay), and power-user extras (Inventory Reconciliation, the word processor, the Email Manager). Registrants receive the full agenda by email closer to the event.
How long is the workshop?
Four hours total, split into two parts with a break in between. Plan to arrive a few minutes early and stay for the full session — the second half builds on the first.
Can I bring my team?
Absolutely. Multi-attendee registrations are encouraged — please register each person separately so we can plan seating and materials accurately.
What should I bring?
A laptop or tablet is helpful but not required. Bring your store's questions, your top workflow headaches, and a willingness to ask in front of peers — that's where the best conversations happen.
How will I know if anything changes?
Anyone registered will receive email updates as venue details, agenda items, and timing get confirmed. You'll always have the latest information before you travel.
